\# IDEA Money Management EA



A professional MetaTrader 5 Expert Advisor for money management and risk control, developed by Omran IDEA.



\## Features

\- Line-based position entry (drag Entry, SL, TP lines on chart)

\- Automatic lot size calculation based on risk %

\- Two risk-free modes (Risk-Free \& Break-Even)

\- Percentage-based partial close

\- On-chart control panel

\- Economic calendar integration

\- Max daily loss protection



\## Versions

| Version | Platform |

|---------|----------|

| 1.x | MetaTrader 5 |



\## Installation

1\. Copy `src/IDEA\_MoneyManagement.mq5` to your MT5 `Experts` folder

2\. Open MetaEditor and compile

3\. Attach to any chart
